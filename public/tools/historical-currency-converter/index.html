What is the Historical Currency Converter?

The Historical Currency Converter by Abetworks is a free tool that lets you check exchange rates from any past date. Whether you need to verify a historical transaction, analyze currency trends, or compare past and present rates, our tool provides accurate historical forex data instantly.

How to Use

  1. Select the source currency from the "From Currency" dropdown
  2. Select the target currency from the "To Currency" dropdown
  3. Choose a historical date using the date picker
  4. Enter the amount you want to convert
  5. Click "Get Historical Rate" to see the conversion
  6. Compare with current rates and export results as PDF

Features

  • ✅ Historical exchange rates for any past date
  • ✅ Support for 20+ major world currencies
  • ✅ Side-by-side comparison with current rates
  • ✅ Rate change percentage calculation
  • ✅ Visual bar chart comparison
  • ✅ Export results as PDF
  • ✅ No signup required, completely free

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Historical Currency Converter free to use?

Yes, the Historical Currency Converter by Abetworks is 100% free with no usage limits or signup required.

How far back can I get historical rates?

Our tool provides historical exchange rates going back several years for most major currency pairs.

How often are exchange rates updated?

Historical rates are from end-of-day data. Current rates are updated daily from reliable financial sources.
