What is the Math Equation Solver?

The Math Equation Solver by Abetworks is a powerful, free online calculator that solves mathematical equations with detailed step-by-step solutions. Perfect for students, teachers, and professionals, it handles algebra, calculus, trigonometry, and more with instant results and visual graph representation.

How to Use

  1. Enter your equation in the input field using standard math notation
  2. Use ^ for exponents (e.g., x^2 for x²), sqrt() for square root
  3. Click "Solve Equation" to get step-by-step solutions
  4. View the detailed breakdown of each solving step
  5. Click "Show Graph" to visualize the function
  6. Copy or export your solution as PDF

Supported Equation Types

  • ✅ Linear equations (e.g., 2x + 5 = 15)
  • ✅ Quadratic equations (e.g., x² + 5x + 6 = 0)
  • ✅ Polynomial equations (e.g., x³ - 2x² + x - 2 = 0)
  • ✅ Systems of equations
  • ✅ Trigonometric equations (e.g., sin(x) = 0.5)
  • ✅ Radical equations (e.g., √x = 4)
  • ✅ Exponential and logarithmic equations
