What is the Bulk QR Code Generator?

Our free Bulk QR Code Generator lets you create multiple QR codes simultaneously. Simply paste up to 50 URLs or text entries (one per line), choose your preferred size, and generate all QR codes in seconds. Preview them in a organized grid layout and download everything as a convenient ZIP file. Perfect for marketing campaigns, event management, product labeling, and batch processing needs.

How to Use

  1. Paste your URLs or text content in the input area (one entry per line)
  2. Select your preferred QR code size (150x150 to 400x400 pixels)
  3. Choose the content type (URL, text, email, phone, or WiFi)
  4. Click "Generate All QR Codes" to create your QR codes
  5. Preview all QR codes in the grid and download as ZIP or individually
