What is the Bulk URL Shortener?

Our free Bulk URL Shortener lets you shorten multiple URLs simultaneously using popular services like is.gd and TinyURL. Simply paste up to 50 long URLs (one per line), choose your preferred shortener, and get all shortened links in seconds. Download results as CSV for spreadsheets or TXT for simple lists. Perfect for social media management, marketing campaigns, affiliate link management, and batch URL processing.

How to Use

  1. Paste your long URLs in the input area (one URL per line)
  2. Select your preferred shortener service (is.gd or TinyURL)
  3. Click "Shorten All URLs" to process your links
  4. Review the shortened URLs and copy or download as needed
  5. Export as CSV for spreadsheets or TXT for simple lists
