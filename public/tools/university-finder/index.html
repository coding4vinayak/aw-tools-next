What is the University Finder?

The University Finder by Abetworks is a free, comprehensive directory tool that helps you search and discover universities worldwide. Powered by the Hipolabs University API, our tool provides access to over 10,000 universities across different countries. Filter by country, search by name, and find state/province-specific institutions with direct links to university websites.

How to Use

  1. Select a country from the dropdown menu
  2. Optionally enter a university name or keyword
  3. Add state/province for more specific results
  4. Click "Search Universities" to find institutions
  5. Browse results and visit university websites
  6. Export results as CSV for your records
