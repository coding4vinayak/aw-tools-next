What is the Competitor App Tracker?

The Competitor App Tracker is a free tool that helps you monitor and compare multiple mobile apps across iOS and Android platforms. Track rating changes, review count growth, price updates, and compare features side by side. Perfect for app developers, product managers, marketers, and investors who need to stay on top of the competitive landscape.

How to Use

  1. Select your platform (iOS or Android)
  2. Search for an app by name and click "Search & Add"
  3. Add up to 10 competitor apps to track
  4. View real-time app statistics and metrics
  5. Compare features in the comparison table
  6. Monitor alerts for rating and price changes
  7. Export comparison data as PDF or CSV

Features

  • ✅ Track up to 10 competitor apps
  • ✅ iOS App Store integration (iTunes API)
  • ✅ Manual entry for Android apps
  • ✅ Rating and review count monitoring
  • ✅ Price change detection
  • ✅ Feature comparison table
  • ✅ Alert notifications for changes
  • ✅ Export reports as PDF or CSV
  • ✅ No signup required, completely free

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Competitor App Tracker completely free?

Yes, the Competitor App Tracker by Abetworks is 100% free with no usage limits or signup required.

Which app stores are supported?

This tool supports both iOS App Store (via iTunes API) and can track Android apps through manual data entry for comparison.

How many apps can I track?

You can track up to 10 competitor apps simultaneously and compare them in a feature comparison table.

Can I export the comparison data?

Yes! You can export the app comparison as PDF or CSV for presentations and reports.
