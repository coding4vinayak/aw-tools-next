What is the Robot Avatar Generator?

The Robot Avatar Generator by Abetworks is a free, AI-powered tool that creates unique robot avatars from any text input like names, emails, or usernames. Powered by the DiceBear API, our tool generates deterministic, high-quality avatars perfect for profiles, placeholders, or fun projects. Choose from multiple robot styles and customize your avatar instantly.

How to Use

  1. Enter a name, email, or any text as the seed
  2. Select your preferred robot avatar style
  3. Choose the avatar size (100x100 to 500x500)
  4. Pick a background color or use transparent
  5. Click "Generate Robot Avatar" to create your avatar
  6. Download as PNG or copy the avatar URL
