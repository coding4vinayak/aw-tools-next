What is the Market Demographics Analyzer?

The Market Demographics Analyzer by Abetworks is a free, powerful tool that helps businesses understand their target audience. By analyzing industry, location, product type, and price range, our tool generates comprehensive demographic profiles including age distribution, gender split, income estimates, and geographic distribution. Perfect for marketing teams, startups, and business planners.

How to Use

  1. Enter your target market or industry
  2. Select the target country or region
  3. Choose your product/service type (B2B, B2C, etc.)
  4. Select your price range positioning
  5. Click "Analyze Demographics" to generate insights
  6. Review the comprehensive demographic report
  7. Export or copy the report for your planning
