What is the EU VAT Validator?

The EU VAT Validator by Abetworks is a free, powerful online tool that instantly validates VAT (Value Added Tax) numbers for all 27 European Union member states. Using the official EU VIES (VAT Information Exchange System) database, our tool helps businesses verify the validity of VAT numbers before conducting cross-border transactions within the EU.

How to Use

  1. Select the EU country from the dropdown menu
  2. Enter the VAT number (without country prefix)
  3. Click "Validate VAT Number" to check registration
  4. View validation result with company details if valid
  5. Use the information for your business records

  • ✅ Support for all 27 EU member states

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the EU VAT Validator completely free to use?

Yes, the EU VAT Validator by Abetworks is 100% free with no hidden limits or credit card requirements.

Which countries are supported?

Our VAT validator supports all 27 EU member states including Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, and more.

Is the VAT validation result official?

Yes, validation is performed against the official EU VIES database. Results are authoritative but should be verified for critical transactions.
