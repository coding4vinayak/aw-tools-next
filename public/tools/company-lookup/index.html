What is the Company Lookup?

Our free Company Lookup tool provides instant access to comprehensive business intelligence data. Simply enter a company domain or name to retrieve detailed information including company size, industry, location, description, logo, founding year, social media profiles, and contact details. Perfect for sales prospecting, market research, competitive analysis, and business development.

How to Use

  1. Enter a company domain (e.g., google.com) or company name in the search field
  2. Click "Lookup Company" to fetch the business data
  3. Review the comprehensive company profile including size, industry, and location
  4. Copy the company data or explore social profiles and tech stack

Features

  • ✅ Search by domain or company name
  • ✅ Company logo and branding assets
  • ✅ Industry classification and company type
  • ✅ Employee count and company size
  • ✅ Location, revenue, and founding year
  • ✅ Social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, etc.)
  • ✅ Technology stack detection
  • ✅ Contact information (email, phone)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Company Lookup completely free?

Yes, the Company Lookup by Abetworks is 100% free with no usage limits or signup required.

What information can I get about a company?

You can get company name, domain, industry, size, location, description, logo, founding year, social media profiles, and contact information.

Yes, you can search using either a company domain (e.g., google.com) or company name (e.g., Google Inc).
