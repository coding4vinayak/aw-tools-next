What is the URL Expander?

Our free URL Expander is a powerful security and productivity tool that reveals the original destination of shortened URLs. Before clicking on suspicious links from emails, social media, or messaging apps, use this tool to see where they really lead. View the complete redirect chain, check safety status, and protect yourself from phishing attacks and malicious websites.

How to Use

  1. Copy the shortened URL you want to expand (from bit.ly, tinyurl, t.co, etc.)
  2. Paste it into the input field above
  3. Click "Expand URL" to reveal the destination
  4. Review the expanded URL, safety status, and redirect chain
  5. Copy the expanded URL or visit it safely if it's legitimate

Features

  • ✅ Supports all major URL shorteners (bit.ly, tinyurl, t.co, goo.gl, is.gd, and more)
  • ✅ Displays complete redirect chain showing all intermediate URLs
  • ✅ Safety check to identify potentially malicious links
  • ✅ One-click copy to clipboard for expanded URLs
  • ✅ Direct visit option with safety confirmation
  • ✅ Detailed URL analysis including domain and protocol info
  • ✅ No signup required, completely free and unlimited
