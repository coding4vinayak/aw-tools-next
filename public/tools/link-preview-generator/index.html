What is the Link Preview Generator?

The Link Preview Generator by Abetworks is a free, powerful online tool that instantly generates visual previews for any website URL. Extract title, description, images, and metadata to see exactly how your links will appear when shared on social media, messaging apps, or email. Perfect for marketers, content creators, and developers.

How to Use

  1. Enter the full URL of the website you want to preview
  2. Click "Generate Preview" to fetch the link metadata
  3. View the visual preview card showing title, description, and image
  4. Review extracted metadata including Open Graph tags
  5. Copy metadata or title to clipboard for your use

Features

  • ✅ Instant link preview generation
  • ✅ Visual preview card (like social media)
  • ✅ Extract Open Graph metadata
  • ✅ Get title, description, and images
  • ✅ Copy metadata to clipboard
  • ✅ Works with most websites
  • ✅ No signup required, completely free

Benefits

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Link Preview Generator completely free to use?

Yes, the Link Preview Generator by Abetworks is 100% free with no hidden limits or credit card requirements.

What websites are supported for link previews?

Our tool works with most public websites that have proper Open Graph or meta tags. Some sites may block preview generation.

How accurate are the link previews?

Previews are generated from the website's actual metadata. Accuracy depends on how well the target site implements Open Graph tags.
