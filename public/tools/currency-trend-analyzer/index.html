What is the Currency Trend Analyzer?

The Currency Trend Analyzer by Abetworks is a powerful free tool that helps you visualize and analyze currency pair trends over time. Track 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day trends with interactive charts, detailed statistics, and simple moving average predictions to inform your currency decisions.

How to Use

  1. Select your base currency (the currency you're converting from)
  2. Select your target currency (the currency you're converting to)
  3. Click "Analyze Trend" to load the trend data
  4. Switch between 30, 60, and 90-day views using the period buttons
  5. Review statistics including high, low, average, and volatility
  6. Check the trend prediction and export data if needed

Features

  • ✅ Multi-period analysis: 30, 60, and 90 days
  • ✅ Interactive trend chart visualization
  • ✅ Key statistics: high, low, average, change, volatility
  • ✅ Simple Moving Average (SMA) prediction
  • ✅ Trend direction indicator (up/down/stable)
  • ✅ Export data as CSV for further analysis
  • ✅ Copy analysis summary to clipboard
  • ✅ No signup required, completely free

Understanding the Statistics

Period High/Low

The highest and lowest exchange rates during the selected period. These values help identify the trading range and potential support/resistance levels.

Period Average

The mean exchange rate over the period. Current rates above average suggest an uptrend, while rates below suggest a downtrend.

Volatility

Measured as the standard deviation of daily changes. Higher volatility indicates more price fluctuation and potentially higher risk/opportunity.
