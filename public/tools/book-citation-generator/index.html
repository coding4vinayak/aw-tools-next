What is the Book Citation Generator?

The Book Citation Generator by Abetworks is a free, powerful tool that helps students, researchers, and academics generate accurate book citations in multiple styles. Search by ISBN or title using the Google Books API, and instantly get properly formatted citations in APA (7th edition), MLA (9th edition), or Chicago/Turabian (17th edition) styles. Perfect for essays, research papers, and bibliographies.

How to Use

  1. Select search type (ISBN or Title)
  2. Enter ISBN number or book title
  3. Click "Search Book" to find the book
  4. Review book details and cover
  5. Select citation style (APA, MLA, or Chicago)
  6. Copy the citation or export as TXT

Features

  • ✅ Search by ISBN-10 or ISBN-13
  • ✅ Search by book title
  • ✅ APA 7th edition citations
  • ✅ MLA 9th edition citations

  • ✅ Book cover preview

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this book citation generator free?

Yes, this citation generator is 100% free with unlimited use. Powered by the Google Books API.

What citation styles are supported?

This tool supports APA (7th edition), MLA (9th edition), and Chicago/Turabian (17th edition) citation styles.

Can I search by ISBN?

Yes, you can search by ISBN-10, ISBN-13, or by book title and author name.
