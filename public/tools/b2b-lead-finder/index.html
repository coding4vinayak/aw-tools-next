What is the B2B Lead Finder?

The B2B Lead Finder by Abetworks is a free, powerful tool designed to help sales and marketing teams discover potential business leads. Filter by industry, company size, location, and keywords to find relevant companies for your outreach campaigns. Export your leads as CSV for easy integration with your CRM or sales pipeline.

How to Use

  1. Select your target industry from the dropdown
  2. Filter by company size (employee count)
  3. Choose geographic location
  4. Add optional keywords for refined search
  5. Click "Find B2B Leads" to discover companies
  6. Review leads and export as CSV for your CRM

Features

  • ✅ Industry-based filtering (14+ industries)
  • ✅ Company size filtering (1-10 to 1000+ employees)
  • ✅ Geographic location filtering (10+ countries)
  • ✅ Keyword-based search refinement
  • ✅ Company details display
  • ✅ Website and contact information
  • ✅ Export leads as CSV
  • ✅ No signup required
  • ✅ Free unlimited searches
