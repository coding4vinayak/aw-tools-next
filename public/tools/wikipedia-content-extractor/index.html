What is the Wikipedia Content Extractor?

The Wikipedia Content Extractor by Abetworks is a free, powerful online tool that extracts full content from Wikipedia articles using the official Wikipedia API. Get article summaries, full text, sections, infobox data, images, and references instantly. Perfect for research, content analysis, and knowledge extraction.

How to Use

  1. Enter the Wikipedia article title
  2. Click "Fetch Article" to retrieve content
  3. View article summary, infobox data, and full content
  4. Review extracted sections and references
  5. Copy content or export as TXT/Markdown
